#VarDOQUERY
---+++ DOQUERY
Combines a !FormQueryPlugin query and showing the results of that query, <code>%DOQUERY</code> accepts all the same parameters as <code>%FORMQUERY</code> and <code>%SHOWQUERY</code> (except =name= and =query=).
